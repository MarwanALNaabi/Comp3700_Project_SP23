Sport Field Booking

We are Three student from Sultan Qaboos University developing Sport Field Booking website.

The main objective of our website is to give the clients the opportunity to reserve from any club using the site, to compare the prices between different clubs, gather the clubs in one website, facilitate the booking process, and use a loyalty program. Through the browser, the user will have an account that will help him to book and cancel a reservation. And, add a club into the website.
